SIRNICA

Sirnica is my favorite pie and it's made with cheese. It can be made as small shell shapes or one big coil or layered

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of smooth flour
  • 1 egg white
  • 4 spoons of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 260 ml lukewarm water
  • 500 grams of fresh cheese
  • 150 g of yogurt
  • 3 eggs
  • salt as muc as you want

Steps

  1. Add the oil to the shifted flour and so on and soak well by hand. Gradually add water and mix a very smooth dough - to appear in the bladder.
  2. Coat with oil, cover the bowl and let he dough to res.
  3. For stuffing, mix all the above ingredients andd salt as needed.
  4. Grow the dough on the flour-stretch desk and switch to cloth sheet. Spread the oil and let it stand to easily stretch.
  5. Stretch and spread with stuffing on the edges.
  6. Bend int o rolls and hang as you wish. Put it in the round pan and start making rolls from middle to out.
  7. Drip them with a little oil again
  8. When it's over we usually eat with cream on top or yogurt
